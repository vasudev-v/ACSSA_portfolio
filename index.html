about our association

🎯

our mission

to create a supportive community for computer science students at the university of winnipeg, fostering academic excellence and professional development.

👁️

our vision

to be a catalyst for innovation and collaboration, preparing students to become leaders in the ever-evolving field of computer science and technology.

💎

our values

we believe in inclusivity, continuous learning, ethical computing practices, and building meaningful connections within our tech community.

what we do

🛠️

workshops & hackathons

we organize regular coding workshops, hackathons, and tech talks to enhance practical skills and foster innovation.

🚀

career development

we connect students with industry professionals through networking events, resume workshops, and internship opportunities.

📚

academic support

we provide study groups, tutoring sessions, and resources to help students excel in their computer science courses.

🤝

community building

we host social events, game nights, and team-building activities to create a supportive and inclusive community.
